About the Story

Pokemon: The Origin of Species is a rationalist take on the Pokemon universe, exploring what would happen if the world operated on consistent, discoverable rules.

Follow Red, Blue, and Leaf as they pursue their ambitions in a world where Pokemon are dangerous, vulnerable, and scientifically fascinating creatures.

Status: Ongoing

Genre: Rationalist Fiction, Pokemon Fanfic, Sci-Fi
