About Rational Fiction

"Rational fiction" is fiction that makes a deliberate effort to reward a reader's thinking. The worldbuilding and plot are intended to stand up to careful thought, the heroes generally solve their problems through careful reasoning first and foremost, and a clever reader should be able to deduce the same solutions the protagonists end up using.
